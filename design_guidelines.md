# Design Guidelines for Swamy Ads Landing Page

## Design Approach
**Reference-Based Approach**: Drawing inspiration from professional service websites like agency portfolios and corporate sites, emphasizing trust, credibility, and visual impact for outdoor advertising showcase.

## Core Design Elements

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Deep Navy: 220 85% 15% (professional authority)
- Warm Orange: 25 90% 55% (energy, visibility - complementing logo)

**Supporting Colors:**
- Light Gray: 210 15% 95% (backgrounds)
- Dark Gray: 220 15% 25% (text)
- White: 0 0% 100% (clean sections)

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Google Fonts) - clean, professional
- **Headings**: Font weights 600-700, sizes from text-2xl to text-5xl
- **Body**: Font weight 400-500, text-base to text-lg
- **Accent Text**: Font weight 500 for emphasis

### Layout System
**Spacing Framework**: Tailwind units of 4, 8, 12, and 16 for consistent rhythm
- Sections: py-16 to py-24
- Component spacing: gap-8, gap-12
- Inner content: p-8, p-12

### Visual Treatment
**Gradients**: Subtle navy-to-darker-navy gradients for section backgrounds
**Background Treatments**: Clean white sections alternating with light gray, hero with subtle gradient overlay
**Contrast Strategy**: High contrast for readability, strategic color placement for CTAs

## Component Library

### Navigation
- Clean horizontal nav with company logo
- Smooth scroll links to sections
- Mobile hamburger menu

### Hero Section
- Large hero image showcasing outdoor advertising work
- Company logo prominently displayed
- Compelling headline about outdoor advertising leadership
- Primary CTA button with warm orange background

### Core Sections (Maximum 5)
1. **Hero**: Company introduction with striking visuals
2. **Services**: Grid layout showcasing advertising services
3. **About/Mission-Vision**: Company story and values
4. **Portfolio**: Client showcase and previous works combined
5. **Contact**: Location details and contact information

### UI Elements
- **Cards**: Clean white backgrounds with subtle shadows
- **Buttons**: Primary (orange), Secondary (outline navy)
- **Portfolio Items**: Image-first cards with overlay text
- **Icons**: Heroicons for services and contact details

## Images
**Hero Image**: Large banner image (required) showing impressive outdoor advertising installations or billboards
**Portfolio Images**: High-quality photos of previous advertising campaigns and client work
**Service Icons**: Simple iconography representing different advertising services
**Company Photos**: Professional images of the team or office if available

## Layout Principles
- **Single-page scroll design** with smooth navigation
- **Mobile-first responsive** approach
- **Generous whitespace** for professional feel
- **Visual hierarchy** through typography and spacing
- **Trust-building elements** through testimonials and portfolio showcase

## Key Considerations
- Emphasize **credibility and professionalism** for B2B clients
- Showcase **visual impact** of outdoor advertising work
- Maintain **local market connection** (Rajahmundry heritage)
- **Fast loading** optimized for mobile users
- **Clear contact pathways** for lead generation

This design approach balances professional corporate aesthetics with the visual nature of advertising work, creating a trustworthy yet impactful online presence for Swamy Ads.
